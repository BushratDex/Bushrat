Bushrat Token

BushRat token is a deflationary token on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) Network with a multi chain
feature and a bridge to connect with other network.
Low trading fees
Bushrat Ecosystem is an highly incentivised protocol that rewards holders and ensures low fees for trades.
Lending and Borrowing
Bushrat Ecosystem provides you with opportunity to borrow and lend tokens out in order to make more profit.
NFT Market Place
Bushrat Ecosystem also comprise of an NFT Market Place that promotes African NFTs and Artists.
Play2Earn Gaming Platform
Bushrat Ecosystem also provides a platform for gamers to earn while they play various games available on the platform.
